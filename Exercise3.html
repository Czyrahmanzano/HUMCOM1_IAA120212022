How to cook Fried Chicken

Ingredients
  • Chicken
  • 2 cups of Flour
  • 1/2 cup of Crispy Fry
  • 2 tbps Garlic Powder
  • 1 tbsp Paprica
  • 1 tsp Pepper
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 cup of Cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp Onion Powder
  • 2 Eggs
  • Oil

Procedure
  1. Prepare ingredients.
  2. Wash chicken.
  3. In a bowl, mix all dry ingredients.
  4. In a separate bowl, crack eggs and beat it.
  5. Prepare a plate to place the breaded chicken.
  6. Get your chicken and dip it in the egg mixture then dip it in the flour mixture.
  7. Make sure to coat boat sides of the chicken.
  8. Put it in the respective platee.
  9. Repeat the sequence until you have coated all of your chicken.
  10. In a pan, heat oil in the right temperature for frying.
  11. When oil is ready, put chicken several pieces at a time.
  12. Fry your chicken until golden brown.
  13. Place paper towel on a plate to drain the excess oil.
  14. Place chicken on the plate.
  15. Enjoy your dish.

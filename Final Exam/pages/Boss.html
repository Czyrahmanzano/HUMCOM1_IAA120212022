✧ Dvalin ✧

Dvalin, also known as Stormterror, is an Anemo elemental dragon and the original Dragon of the East of Mondstadt's Four Winds.
He has been the Anemo Archon Barbatos' friend for almost 2000 years and is the longest-worshipped of the Four Winds.

✧ Andrius ✧

Andrius is known as the King of the North Wind as well as the Lupus Boreas. In Greek mythology, Boreas is known as the god of the north wind.
He is the purple-winged god of the north wind and one of the four seasonal Anemoi, which are the Wind Gods.

✧ Childe ✧

"Childe" Tartaglia, Eleventh of the Fatui Harbingers. He draws power from the ominous Delusion he possesses and fights using martial arts that he learned in the land of darkness.

✧ Azhdaha ✧

Azhdaha was unable to unseal itself in the end, and the quaking earth was at last put to rest.
Yet the memories of facing the sealed dragon in the heart of the earth itself remain ever so vivid.

✧ Signora ✧

The Raiden Shogun's judgment has descended, and the Fatuus Signora shall trouble the Seven Nations no longer.
Yet the memories of climbing the steps of Tenshukaku and engaging her in duel before the throne are still fresh in your mind.

✧ Raiden Shogun ✧

A will that surpasses time itself finally caused her to step aside and allow Eternity to enter a new chapter.
But behold, that thrilling battle at Mt. Yougou has been revived in the form of memories.
